Content Engine.

A unified generative copywriting schedule platform designed to research, draft, structure, and queue multi-channel marketing campaigns.

View GitHub Repository

Overview

Maintaining marketing cadence across multiple digital channels represents a significant operations drag for early-stage companies. Founders and marketing directors often spend 15+ hours weekly researching topics, writing drafts, and scheduling them across platforms. We built Content Engine to centralize this entire pipeline.

Project Type

SaaS MVP

Timeline

4 Weeks

Core Outcome

340+ articles generated

Client State

Active MVP Launch

The Problem

Creating content consistently is difficult. Traditional content calendars hold posts but do not help write them. In contrast, standard copywriting AI tools output generic templates that lack company voice consistency and fail to link directly with social APIs. Founders get bogged down copying and pasting drafts between tools.

Discovery Workshop

We found that user engagement increases when articles are structured as structural outlines rather than fully automated text. Presenting founders with a researched outline that they can edit and refine led to higher publication rates and real narrative quality.

Product Thinking

We structured the content engine around outlines rather than raw text generation. Handing founders a structural guide to iterate on ensures that the final published copy retains real personal insight and narrative tone, increasing conversions.

Design & UX

We designed a minimalist split editor. The left side controls the keyword outline structures, and the right provides a clean typography text editor that directly updates calendar delivery tracks.

System Architecture

The engine links keyword search metrics to an LLM writing pipeline. The system utilizes structured prompt models to ingest company background context, matches it to current industry trends, drafts content trees, and delivers them directly into a drag-and-drop Kanban interface queue connected to social API endpoints.

Tech Stack

React.js Node.js OpenAI API Prisma ORM PostgreSQL LinkedIn & Twitter API Tailwind CSS

Key Features

  • ⚡ Kanban Queue: Interactive schedule management with drag-and-drop blocks.
  • ⚡ Content Outline Builder: Auto-generates structured headers and key talking points.
  • ⚡ API Publish Hooks: Single-click scheduling directly to LinkedIn and blogs.
  • ⚡ Trend Telemetry: Automatically audits organic search volume metrics.

Engineering Challenges

Maintaining narrative coherence across multiple channel posts required careful state management. Generative queries often hallucinated details or diverged from configured customer profiles. We solved this by implementing strict context window boundaries and utilizing validation checks that audit final drafts before release queues.

Outcome

Our customers saw a 16.4% MoM traffic growth during early validation. Content Engine generated more than 345 publishing-approved drafts, reducing founder copywriting overhead from 15 hours to 2 hours weekly.

Future Roadmap

We are deploying semantic scraping agents designed to follow specific LinkedIn/Twitter discussions, automatically suggesting contextual replies or brand outreach opportunities to target leads.
